TPM Consultancy

Total Productive Maintenance, shortly termed as TPM is adopted to achieve high level of productivity. TPM is based on zero-loss concept viz., zero break downs, zero accidents, zero defect etc., primarily to achieve high reliability/flexibility of equipment and reduce costs through minimising wastage of man hours, raw material, power, tools etc. Our Team welcomes challenging projects from our clients and delivers through total participation of all people inside the organisation and then developing self -managing abilities in people and practices.

As a proof of quality of our service, the consultancy fee that we charge is a percentage of profitability or cost reduction achieved through our intervention.

Who We Are

While the government owns the primary responsibility for the development of the society, active and deliberate participation from the private sector and industries is the key to make the development rapid and sustainable. More and more corporate are acknowledging this responsibility every day with clear intent to give back to the society. Unfortunately, most of these good intentions, by large, remain to be wishful thinking due to lack of actual know-how, right partners and poor strategy or execution.

Grassroot Impact(GI) is team of dedicated professionals with diverse backgrounds - from manufacturing industries and corporates to development sector, who are committed to help industries create an over-all positive and sustainable impact within and outside!

Vision

"To help socially responsible organizations invest and participate in the right initiatives with meaningful, measurable and lasting impact on itself and the society."

Mission

"To benefit 1 million lives through GI initiatives by providing access to employable skills, quality education, meaningful livelihoods and clean energy."
